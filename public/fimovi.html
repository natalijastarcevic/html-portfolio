The Best Movies According to Natalija

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Mirror

It explores themes of memory, identity, and the passage of time, blending personal and historical elements.

The Usual Suspects

The film's ending is particularly iconic and has become one of the most talked about twist endings in cinematic history.

One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest

The performances in the film, especially by Jack Nicholson and Louise Fletcher, are widely praised.
